About

Ever gotten tired of the hassle to find school websites? The long URLs, the slow loading websites? Well, now you can Take control of your academic life with this game-changing Chrome extension. Effortlessly access your school portals, organize assignments with custom tasks, and add personalized portals for quick, easy access to the resources you need. Built with students in mind, it’s designed to simplify your daily routine and keep you ahead of the curve.
